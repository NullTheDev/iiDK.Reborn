## About

**iiDK.Reborn** is the maintenance fork of iiDK’s original mod menu for Gorilla Tag. I’m not rewriting it—just keeping it **stable, patched, and usable** while the community enjoys it.  

### Why Open Source?

Open source keeps Gorilla Tag modding **free, collaborative, and educational**. Everyone can learn, experiment, and contribute, without paywalls or hidden code.
